Microsoft® Close Combat
Point and click and boom! the shooting starts the second your men step off Omaha Beach...

In Close Combat, everything reacts like it does on the battlefield, from the ripping canvas hiss of the German MG42 machine gun to the screams of the men it hits. Each soldier has a psychological profile that affects his behavior on the battleground. And all the combat action happens right in front of you, in real time. Troops, terrain, situations—if you want to get any more accurate, you'll have to shave your head! You're in command, but your men have minds of their own..

  • With Close Combat you command soldiers who dynamically react to the situation they're in based upon their strength, ability, stamina, and mental condition.
  • Experience the heat of battle with continuous, real-time action. Great graphics, animation, and sound effects help make the battlefield come alive as you command the Americans or the Germans.
  • See how well you command under the same conditions faced by WWII commanders as you fight through historically accurate battle situations.
  • You can even go head-to-head in two-player mode.
