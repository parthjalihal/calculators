The smartest way to buy your next car.

With the Microsoft CarPoint online automotive service, you're armed with the straightforward, up-to-date, and objective information you need to save time and money to make the best NEW car purchase decision. Since CarPoint is on the World Wide Web, it's more current than books or CD-ROMs. Plus, Microsoft is constantly expanding and updating CarPoint, so there's always something new to see.
