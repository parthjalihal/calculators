Microsoft® My Personal Tutor
Personalized learning that unlocks your child’s potential. Instruction that’s right on target.

Microsoft My Personal Tutor is a comprehensive learning solution for preschool to first grade. With TutorAssist™ learning technology, My Personal Tutor provides targeted instruction when and where your child needs it most. With My Personal Tutor, your child holds the key to learning fun.


Recommended for kids ages 3-7

  • TutorAssist™ learning technology - TutorAssist assesses your child’s learning progress and offers targeted instruction when and where it is needed, helping your child achieve new levels of learning.
  • Professor Presto - This engaging character is right there to explain the concepts behind problems, so that your child learns new material instead of simply practicing what he or she already knows.
  • Comprehensive learning - Four activity-packed compact discs cover essential math, reading, and preschool skills with in-depth content.
  • Hours of learning fun - Hundreds of activities provide hours of enjoyment.
  • Parent progress report - Track your child’s progress across all learning objectives and topic areas in a single, cross-subject view.
  • Supplemental educational materials - The Learning Activity Guide and Read-With-Me Coloring Book offer continued learning away from the PC.
