Scholastic's The Magic School Bus® Explores in the Age of Dinosaurs
A Fun-Filled, Fact-Packed Science Adventure! Bump into a Barosaurus. Rub elbows with a Stegosaurus. Even outrun a Tyrannosaurus Rex!

Inspired by Scholastic, Inc's award-winning book series and hit PBS TV show, this CD-ROM software brings science concepts to life in a way that entertains, fascinates, and encourages exploration.


Recommended for kids ages 6-10
Microsoft Kids Homepage

Video
  • Travel to seven different dinosaur hot spots in three prehistoric eras: from the Triassic to the Jurassic to the Cretaceous. Then hop out of the bus and explore. In your travels you hunt for the lost dinosaur snapshots from Ms. Frizzle's photo journal...and become a dino expert!
  • You'll become a dinosaur (and plant and reptile) expert in no time - and have fun too - with tons of games, activities, videos, reports, fun facts, and clickable animations throughout the product.
  • Dinosaurs needed to protect their nests from predators...now you can too when you play the nest watch game. Or help a dino escape from T-Rex; practice your paleontology with the footprint puzzle; be a game show star (and show your smarts) in Dino-Madness; or send "past cards" to your friends and family from your visit to the land of dinosaurs.
