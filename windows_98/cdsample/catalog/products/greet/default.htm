Microsoft® Greetings Workshop
Create the Quality of Hallmark at Home

Greetings Workshop is an award-winning home desktop publishing application from Microsoft and Hallmark Connections™, the technology brand of Hallmark Cards, Inc. You can create Hallmark-quality cards, invitations, banners, posters, calendars and more to be printed or sent over the Internet.
Greetings Workshop Homepage

Video
  • Hallmark Connections content - Choose from more than 10,000 ready-to-personalize projects or create your own from scratch. With more than 10,000 graphics, 500 photos, 125 fonts, backgrounds, borders and more, there is no end to your creativity.
  • Easy-to-use - Anyone, even first time users, can easily create projects with step-by-step help from start to finish including printing two-sided projects or sending your card via e-mail.
  • Creative Flexibility - Personalize the projects you create with special text and graphics effects or by adding personal photos. Use new drawing tools to add shapes and lines to your projects.
  • Card Stock and Envelopes - Add a finishing touch to your projects with the Hallmark Connections card stock and matching envelopes included in the box.
  • Endless Creativity - Create a Hallmark-quality including cards, invitations, announcements, banners, posters, stationery, label, stickers, calendars, awards and more for any occasion.
  • Many Ways to Share - Print your personalized creations or send them via e-mail. Whichever way you choose, you'll be able to share with friends and family anywhere.
  • Never Forget a Special Occasion - Greetings Workshop reminders help you keep track of important dates and events.
