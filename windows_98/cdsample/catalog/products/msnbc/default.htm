MSNBC® on the Internet
MSNBC on the Internet offers consumers a wealth of interactive services related to news and information.

MSNBC is an Internet news service that complements the MSNBC Cable Network, a joint venture between Microsoft Corp. and NBC. This interactive service provides in-depth reporting and information on a wide range of news topics, from local, to national, to international news.
MSNBC on the Internet

Video
  • MSNBC offers consumers local, national and international news and information using text, audio and video.
  • NBC News anchors, correspondents, and reporters make regular appearances online, either through special chat events or in news segments produced specifically for MSNBC on the Internet. Additionally, the service employs a number of journalists dedicated to providing comprehensive news coverage online.
  • MSNBC on the Internet offers consumers a variety of tools and services to personalize their use of the service.
  • Hypertext links throughout the service's content takes viewers to other areas of MSNBC as well as to other Internet sites relevant to their topics of interest.
