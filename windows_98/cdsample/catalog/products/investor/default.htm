Microsoft® Investor
Microsoft Investor (a web based service) provides the information today's investors need.

Microsoft Investor offers such tools and information as personalized business and financial news, a portfolio manager, charting, historical data, market summaries, quote look-up, and online trading. Microsoft Investor helps people organize, follow and understand their investments and investment decisions.


Microsoft Investor
  • Portfolio Manager - Keeps investors up-to-date with current stock information, and allows you to create and track multiple stock portfolios. The Portfolio Manager incorporates stock splits, multiple purchase dates, and much more. Plus, it automatically alerts investors when there is news on any stock in their portfolio.
  • Company News - Enables investors to garner critical market information, including up-to-the-minute business and investment news. The Company News feature helps investors understand why their stocks might be rising or falling.
  • Historical Charts - Investors can study split-adjusted history charts for their securities. They can choose the time period, overlay market indexes and other securities, customize chart settings, and download historical data to understand the past price performance of their favorite stocks.
  • Market Summary - Provides up-to-the-minute information on the leading U.S. and foreign indexes, currency rates, and the market's ten best and worst performers as measured either by volume or percent gain and loss.
  • Quote Lookup - A tool for site visitors to check current prices and other pertinent details of stocks and mutual funds. Quote details include current price, high, low, volume, EPS, P/E, volatility, outstanding shares, change, percentage change, ask, bid, last trade size, and market capitalization.
  • Online Trading - Investors can save time and reduce commission fees by trading online with the top financial institutions integrated directly with Microsoft Investor.
