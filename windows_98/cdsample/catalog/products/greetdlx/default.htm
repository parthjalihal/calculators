Microsoft® Greetings Workshop Deluxe
The Ultimate Way to Create the Quality of Hallmark at Home!

Greetings Workshop Deluxe is the ultimate way to create Hallmark-quality cards, invitations, banners, posters, calendars and more. Also, create exciting multimedia greetings with music, sound and animation. Print or send your projects over the Internet to friends and family.

  • 2 CDs of Hallmark Connections content- Choose from more than 12,000 ready-to-personalize projects, hundreds of pre-designed multimedia greetings or create your own from scratch. With more than 15,000 graphics, 1,000 photos, 1,000 graphical animations, 500 sounds, 100 music clips, 125 fonts, backgrounds, borders and more, there is no end to your creativity.
  • New Multimedia Greetings - Bring your projects to life with music, sound and animation. Imagine a party invitation with party horns blowing or a holiday greeting with dancing reindeer.
  • Easy-to-use - Anyone, even first time users, can easily create projects with step-by-step help from start to finish including printing two-sided projects or sending your project through e-mail.
  • Creative Flexibility - Personalize the projects you create with special text and graphics effects or by adding personal photos. Use new drawing tools to add shapes and lines to your projects.
  • Card Stock and Envelopes - Add a finishing touch to your projects with the Hallmark Connections card stock and matching envelopes included in the box.
  • Many Ways to Share - Print your personalized creations, post them at the Greetings Workshop post office, send them via e-mail or save them on disk. Whichever way you choose, you'll be able to share with friends and family anywhere. It's easy to do with Internet Explorer and 30 days free access to MSN included in the box.
  • Never Forget a Special Occasion - Greetings Workshop reminders help you keep track of important dates and events.
