Microsoft® Expedia™ travel services
Microsoft Expedia is where your travel begins!

Microsoft Expedia is a free travel service on the World Wide Web and MSN™ (The Microsoft Network). Expedia is the place to go to plan and purchase travel on the Internet. Expedia lets you find low fares, book flights, make hotel reservations, and rent cars. Expedia also gives you access to a comprehensive source of travel information to help you decide where to go and what to do once you get there.


Expedia travel services
  • Fare tracker - A unique e-mail service available at no charge that updates customers on the best fares between the destinations they choose.
  • Seat pinpointer - Choose your own plane seat(s) using a simple graphical map of the inside of the plane. Seats that are reserved will appear in gray and open selections will appear in X.
  • Fare compare - Find the lowest fares using this interactive search that will produce a screen listing fares along with any restrictions that may apply.
  • Flight information - Check the status of your flight before you leave for the airport, making it easy to see if your flight is on time or delayed.
