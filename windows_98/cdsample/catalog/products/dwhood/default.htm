DreamWorks Interactive™: The Neverhood
Immerse yourself in a unique clay world!

Born into a Neverhood that may never exist, Klaymen explores a land of terrifying creatures, extraordinary machines and mysterious artifacts. Long ago the king of this world was betrayed by his trusted assistant. You must save the Neverhood by navigating Klaymen through a twisted plot of ancient secrets, witty inventions and monstrous puzzles to defeat your nasty foe.
DreamWorks Interactive - The Neverhood
  • Clever, engaging and challenging. Edgy humor and special effects will keep you hooked for hours.
  • Immerse yourself in a unique world. Explore secret places and hidden objects. Follow clues though caves, trap doors and mysterious hallways which help you answer puzzles on your way to solving the quest.
  • Easy to use. Smoothly integrated - no need to check inventory, location or use a keyboard. No keyboard commands to learn.
  • Point and click you way through more than 60 hilarious and twisted puzzles.
  • Effects created with more than 3 tons of clay and over 50,000 frames of animation.
  • Total immersion. No need to keep track of maps, scores or inventory.
