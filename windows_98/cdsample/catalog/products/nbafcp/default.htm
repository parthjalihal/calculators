NBA Full Court Press™
Experience the thrilling NBA Basketball with crisp arena and player graphics. So real, you'll sweat.

NBA Full Court Press is a five-on-five NBA basketball game for Windows 95 that features thrilling competition, realistic action, and sights and sounds from around the league. NBA Full Court Press provides gamers with real NBA players and official teams. The game's motion-capture player movements and artificial intelligence model were developed with the help of NBA coaches and players. Realistic game play is accompanied by rich statistical information that affects the on-court performances of players and teams.
