Microsoft® Arcade 1.0 for Windows®

Microsoft Arcade brings you the fast-action play of five classic games—right in Windows. You can customize each game, so you can play the way you want to!


Microsoft Games Homepage
  • Missile Command™—Destroy battle bombers, satellites, missiles and smart bombs!
  • Tempest™—Defend yourself against a horde of enemy aliens!
  • Centipede®—You're the exterminator as centipedes, spiders, mushroom-dropping fleas and poisonous scorpions invade your space!
  • Battlezone®—Deflect and destroy enemy tanks, missiles, supertanks and saucers with an eye on the radar and a finger on the firepower!
  • Asteroids®—Warp into hyperspace and shoot a path to the stars as plummeting asteroids head your way and large saucers take aim at your and blast you out of the sky!
