CART Precision Racing™
For PC racing enthusiasts, CART Precision Racing is one of the most realistic racing simulations on a PC.
Microsoft Games Homepage

Video
  • Provides the most realistic racing experience with advanced 4-point vehicle physics model, six degrees of freedom, suspension strut movement and real world steering.
  • Provides enhanced gameplay and features through the photorealistic cockpit, out-the-window 3D graphics, and digitally sampled sound effects.
  • Official CART tracks and the 1997 Season teams.
  • Visit the Racing School where you can obtain racing tips from Bobby Rahal, a three time CART champion. Users will take lessons in the Racing School and can also see tips and video clips from other pro drivers from actual circuits.
  • Download the qualifying times of real cars and compare the professional driver's best laps against your own.
  • Featuring realistic, animated, pit stops based on motion captured movements of real pit workers.
