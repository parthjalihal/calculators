Monster Truck Madness™
Down & Dirty Racing!

Monster Truck Madness is the most radical and outrageous racing game because it sits players behind the steering wheel of the world's most powerful vehicles as they crush, splatter, jump and roll their way to victory. Monster Truck Madness provides an exhilarating off-road driving experience by offering various racing modes across rugged and challenging terrain.


Microsoft Games Homepage
  • 3 skill levels (from rookie to professional) and 3 types of racing (drag, circuit, rally) keep the action hot and the challenge real.
  • Over 12 tracks, each with a different terrain including off-road mud, sand, and water.
  • Pro tips and videos to give you the competitive edge.
  • Your choice of fantastic viewing angles: from behind the wheel of a competitor's rig to the blimp overhead. You can even watch through a race cam that automatically picks the best angles.
  • Relive the spectacular racing moments with instant replays.
