Microsoft® Mouse version 2.0 for Windows®
Designed for comfort, control, and ease of use.

The ergonomically designed Microsoft Mouse is the ultimate pointing device for the Windows operating system. The unique shape of the Microsoft Mouse gives enhanced support to your hand for ergonomic comfort while innovative software enhances your productivity by simplifying repetitive tasks.

  • IntelliPoint Toolbar makes it easier to activate the features you use most.
  • ClickSaver lets you click just once instead of twice.
  • Snap-to automatically moves the cursor to the default button of the dialogue box, and then back to its original position.
  • SmartSpeed adds control and accuracy by slowing down the cursor whenever it's over icons, title bars, scroll bars, and window borders.
  • LineLock locks the cursor in horizontal or vertical motion.
  • By pressing both buttons together you get an additional button that you can assign to common tasks.
