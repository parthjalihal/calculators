Microsoft® Bookshelf® 98
Quick access to everything you need for productive writing!

Microsoft Bookshelf 98 CD-ROM reference library provides all the resources you need to create superb documents, reports, and presentations. Microsoft Bookshelf 98 gives you fast, easy access to 10 of the most popular reference works. Use it to enhance your work with comprehensive, up-to-date information, or even to search the Internet with our unique links to Infoseek®:

Bookshelf Homepage

  • Complete information from 10 popular, newly updated reference works makes it easy to communicate your points more effectively by adding quotations, synonyms, pictures, maps, and more.
  • The QuickShelf™ information retrieval tool gives you one-click access to Microsoft Bookshelf 98 from within any Windows® 95–based program.
  • QuickDefine lets you easily view word definitions without leaving your current program.
  • QuickFootnote saves time in Microsoft Word by automatically footnoting information and adding a supporting citation to the footer.
  • QuickQuote lets you quickly find the ideal remark for that important document.
  • Bookshelf Premier Web Search linked to Infoseek extends your search for information to over 400,000 high-quality internet sites.
  • Quick ZIP Code lets you find ZIP Codes for addresses anywhere in the United States.
  • Improved article indexing places the most important information first as you search.
