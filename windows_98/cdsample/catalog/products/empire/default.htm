Age of Empires™
Choose your favorite form of world domination in an epic game of empire-building and conquest

How will you rule the world? Conquer enemy civilizations? Discover the world’s secrets? Accumulate wealth? As the guiding spirit of a small Stone Age tribe, follow one of these paths to build the greatest civilization on earth.

  • Build your tribe into a powerful, flourishing empire through more than 10,000 years of real-time evolution.
