Microsoft® Encarta® Virtual Globe

The Ultimate Geographic Reference!

Encarta Virtual Globe is the most comprehensive and dynamic world atlas available. Encarta Virtual Globe provides your family with an engaging and unique view of the world by delivering the highest quality, detailed maps, up-to-date statistics and the richest cultural information of any world atlas in any medium.

  • Explore the world and navigate through a dynamic, interactive model of the earth. Zoom from satellite maps to detailed street -level maps of more than 50 cities worldwide.
  • Immerse yourself in geography and culture with over 6,000 comprehensive articles and 5,000 multimedia images, music, sound and video clips.
  • Easily find over 1.2 million places with a world atlas four times more detailed than any printed world atlas available.
  • The Sensation of Flying lets you experience the sensation of literally flying over geographic landmarks and terrain (e.g. Grand Canyon, Greece and Western Turkey, Andes).
  • Over 9,000 Web Links to sites specially selected by our editors.
    A built-in browser lets you view these sites directly from the Encarta Virtual Globe interface.
  • The new Name That Place Game lets you learn about the world in fun and challenging way. Set your level of difficulty and answer questions about names of places and locations of cities, countries and continents.
