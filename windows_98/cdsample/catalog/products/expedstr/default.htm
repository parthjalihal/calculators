Microsoft® Expedia™ Streets 98

Microsoft Expedia Streets takes the guesswork out of getting where you need to go. Whether it's a local client's office or a museum four states away, Streets Plus will enable you to accurately locate street addresses in the U.S.

Locate specific addresses, streets, restaurants, hotels and other points of interest in seconds. Maps are rich in detail and easy to read for painless navigation. You can even maps in a variety of ways with zoom and panning buttons.
