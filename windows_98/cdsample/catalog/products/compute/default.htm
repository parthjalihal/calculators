Microsoft® Computing on MSN™
Computing on MSN, The Microsoft Network, is designed to be a strong technical resource as well as a friendly and helpful service for new computer-users.

Computing on MSN includes the official Web site of the popular "On Computers" radio show, 20 forums devoted to computing issues, and a friendly guide to information on www.microsoft.com. It also features the Computing Concierge service that supplies personal help at any time from within Computing on MSN.


Computing On MSN
  • On Computers - The official Web site of the popular radio show starring Bob O'Donnell and Linda Jacobsen.
  • Forums - More than two dozen topical computing forums provide a wealth of community and information to computer users at all levels of expertise.
  • The Right Click - An electronic magazine that provides personalized daily news, a sympathetic tech support column, and an interactive monthly feature story for the general computer-user.
  • Guide to www.microsoft.com - A pointer to computer and software information on Microsoft.com that is relevant to new computer-users.
  • Personal View - A customizable daily computer information service that aggregates text, audio stories, and computing tips from the leading online news sources.
