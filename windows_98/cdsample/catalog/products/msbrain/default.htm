Scholastic's the Magic School Bus® Explores the Rainforest
A fun-filled, fact-packed science adventure! Be a rainforest detective on Scholastic’s The Magic School Bus!

Take a wild ride through the rainforest—where kids are always in the driver's seat! Ms. Frizzle and her class are bringing the rainforest to life right in the classroom. Travel to the Costa Rican Rainforest to explore—wahoo!! Start exploring the depth and diversity of the rainforest today!
