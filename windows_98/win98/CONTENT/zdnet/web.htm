The Whole Web Catalog!

ZDNet's Whole Web Catalog is the Net's ultimate destination for people interested in learning how to make the most of the Web. Whether you're an enthusiastic surfer or a world-class site builder, you'll find tons of tips and tricks, how-to guides, informational articles, tool collections, and more.

Learn to maximize your Web cruising speed or how to deliver a high-bandwidth professional site in Whole Web Catalog's comprehensive collection of projects. And you never have to worry about finding the information that's right for you, since we categorize everything we do by level of experience, from Webmaster to beginner.

The Latest Technologies

The Web is changing so quickly, it can be tough to keep pace with all the new technologies. That's why ZDNet Web will keep you current with everything you need to know.

What do we cover? The most popular Web technologies including HTML Authoring, VRML, ActiveX, Browsers, Plug-ins, Searching, E-Mail, and more.

Speaking of VRML, don't forget to stop by our VRML zone, ZD3D, which features fly-through VRML worlds.

And the Explorer Zone, everything you need to know about Internet Explorer 4.0.

We've got a major HTML Authoring section in the works, with a complte online HTML reference manual, tons of tips and tricks, lots of related Garages, etc.

The Basics

If you're just starting on the Web, we've got the site for you.

Call it a crash-course resource for beginners. We've got interactive multimedia demonstrations of how the Web works, just for starters.

Then check out our place where we answer all those questions you never thought you'd find answers to.

Finally, our tips and tricks section will ensure you surf like a pro.

Dynamic Downloads

We find the tools that make an Internet users' life a bit easier. Read the reviews, download the tools, then talk about them in our interactive "two cents" area.

These tools cover everything you'll need to make surfing, searching and even building sites as easy as possible.

The Garage

Our garage is just like your garage – the place where you tackle projects you haven't done before.

We figure you have so much fun surfing you'll have even more fun working with the Web. And that's where our Garage comes in.

What can you learn in our Garage? Install your own personal Web server. Animate your own GIFs. Build your own IE push channels. Work with style sheets. Lay out pages with HTML tables. And more.

Net.Politics

The Net is nothing if not political, at least today.

ZDNet Whole Web Catalog gives you comprehensive coverage of the Internet's political arena, so you're an informed user.

What will you read about? Encryption battles. Censorship. Domain name policy. Intellectual property. Interviews with lawmakers. Point/counterpoint discussions on the future of this medium.

If you surf the Web, you can't miss this.
