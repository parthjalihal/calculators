Channel
Hollywood Online uses the IE 4.0 Channel Bar to provide access to the main areas of our site.

The Channel itself has information about new releases of movies and videos.

Also available is the Hollywood Online Ticker, containing our top stories and box office figures for the top 60 movies this week. The ticker can also be installed as a Desktop Component.
Home Page
The Hollywood Online home page is designed to give you easy access to motion picture content. There are three ways to navigate.

The left menu frame indexes Hollywood Online's programs. Click on the text to link to the content.

The center news frame offers links to Hollywood Online's daily news and "hot-picks" of the day.

The right 'grid' frame has visual links to Hollywood Online's programs and promotions.

ShowTimes
A comprehensive national listing of movies which allows users to locate films by time, zip code, city/state or theatre chain.

Users can order tickets over the phone and even see a map to the theatre! An ultra-convenient service which allows you to arrange your evening without leaving your laptop, ShowTimes is the official web site of the National Association of Theatre Owners (NATO)!
Movies on TV
Want to find out about the movies on television in your area today, tomorrow, and for days in the future?

Movies on TV gives you a fast, easy-to-read grid displaying movie times, channels, ratings, and summaries, as well as a link to complete information about the cast and crew.
MovieTalk
What are top movie celebrities saying about their latest movies? What's on the mind of your favorite actor, writer, director or producer? You can hear it all on MovieTalk, which offers a comprehensive array of audio celebrity interviews.

Listen to interviews with actors, writers, directors and producers from the hottest new movies. View a photo and biography of the celebrities and search the archive for past interviews.
Hollywood News
Updated twice a day, Hollywood News features items on all your favorite stars, events coverage, industry news, box office numbers, gossip columns, and more, including breaking news from the Los Angeles Times' Calendar and Company Town sections.

Hollywood News also hosts a weekly film commentary section called Hollywood Roundtable, with seven new features each week, including in-depth news on independent and foreign film, underground film, the history of Hollywood, and exclusive interviews.

Also featuring The Press Room - THE place for the hottest photos and articles about the coolest movie premieres, award ceremonies, film festivals, Hollywood happenings and behind-the-scenes interviews.
MoviePeople
Hollywood Online's searchable database of movie facts, personality profiles, filmographies and personnel descriptions.

More than 131,000 films and 850,000 cast credits are cross-referenced for quick access.

Includes films and personnel from 1895 to the present day. Hollywood Online MoviePeople Database is one of the most complete film databases on any web site.
Specials
Hollywood Online Specials is the place to find additional areas of our unique content. Featuring:

Digital Revolution: Get all the news, info and multimedia for the hottest new laser and DVD titles--only at Digital Revolution!

The official web site of The Hollywood Chamber of Commerce, where you can find out everything about the famous Walk of Fame, the Hollywood Sign, tourism information, and much more

The Buzz Forums - Discuss your favorite movies with film fans from around the world with more than 20 interest groups and growing communities of professionals, friends and aficionados

Hollywood Online Salutes the Academy Awards - our acclaimed web site on the 1996 Oscars containing the best photos on the Web, behind-the-scenes scoops, Oscar trivia and history, and transcripts of our live online conferences with Academy Award nominees

ShoWest - our exclusive coverage of the annual Las Vegas convention where all the stars turn out to promote their brand-new movies -- complete with video clips, audio clips and photos.
Studio Store
The Hollywood Online Studio Store offers a comprehensive array of motion picture and television merchandise.

Find T-shirts, caps, mugs, autographs, photos, posters, videos and other collectibles from your favorite movies. The transactions are secure and merchandise is added daily.
SiteSeer
The ultimate multimedia search at Hollywood Online. A comprehensive search engine which finds the exact movie multimedia you're looking for faster than you dreamed possible.

Users can locate text, photos, sounds, videos, animation (which at Hollywood Online usually means games!), or any combination by selecting a movie name, a person, or any keyword and clicking on SEARCH. Thumbnails and descriptions of all our multimedia offerings on your celebrity or movie of choice will appear immediately, linkable to the actual web pages, of course.
MovieTunes
The Web's leading motion picture soundtrack information resource. Listen to selected tracks from your favorite motion picture soundtracks and tune into Soundtrack of the Week, Box Office Tunes and the user rated MovieTunes Top 20.

The Site also offers What's Cool - a comprehensive listing of film music programs on the radio and a directory of film music concerts throughout the country.

Movie Music News features stories on film music related topics.

Search the entire site or visit the Composers/Fan Club Area to find profiles of your favorite composers.
