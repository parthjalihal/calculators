One Time Reprints

To request permission to reprint a comic or text feature from Andrews McMeel Universal please send the following information:

  1. The comic to be reprinted and the original publication date.
  2. A detailed explanation of how you plan to use this feature.
  3. If reprinting in a publication, the circulation of the publication, the cost of the publication to its readers, and the frequency of the publication.
  4. Whether you are requesting North American or World rights.
  5. Whether you are a for-profit or not-for-profit organization.
  6. A recent copy of your publication.
