Welcome to LiveWired, Wired's custom-built channel on Microsoft Internet Explorer 4.0. Each day we will bring new and exciting images, headlines and animations from the Wired world, including Wired News and HotWired. LiveWired is delivered to your desktop for browsing offline. You can make LiveWired your screensaver. It takes a bit of tweaking, but if you read the screen saver setup instructions, it will only take about three minutes.

  • Toolbar: Provides you with all the navigation necessary to move throughout LiveWired.
  • Date and Millennium Countdown: Counting the seconds to the new millennium.
  • Bullseye: Replays new LiveWired presentations.
  • Links: Occasionally you will come across links within LiveWired. Clicking on these icons will take you online to the related story or Web site.
  • Hold Button: To pause the current screen, simply move your mouse into the button area, no clicking is necessary. Additional content will appear when you do this.
  • Image Thumbnails: Click any thumbnail to view the presentation locked behind it.
  • LiveWired Thumbnail Categories:
    • Top Story: Photo feature
    • Data: Key facts about the world today and tomorrow
    • Face: Quotes from people we pay attention to
    • Tool: Interesting objects for sale or rent
    • Media: Media creatures from games, movies, TV
    • 4Cast: Predictions
    • Ads: Advertisements
