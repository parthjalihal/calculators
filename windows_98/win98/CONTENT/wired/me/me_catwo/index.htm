Carvel Ice Cream Bakery recently queried 1,500 people in shopping malls around the country about what they wished for on their birthdays. Of that sample group, 61 percent of boys under 10 who wished to be a "superhero" chose Catwoman; 57 percent of girls under 10 who wished for the same chose Xena: Warrior Princess.
Source: Jericho Communications Inc. (New York), Carvel Ice Cream Bakery
